How to Prevent Gum Disease

Gum disease can be prevented with good oral hygiene and a healthy lifestyle. Brush your teeth twice a day using fluoride toothpaste and floss daily to remove plaque buildup. Regular dental checkups and professional cleanings help detect early signs of gum issues.
